About CareForYou

Revolutionizing mental healthcare through compassionate therapy and innovative AI technology

Our Mission

At CareForYou, we believe everyone deserves access to quality mental health support. Our mission is to break down barriers to therapy and create a world where mental wellness is prioritized and accessible to all.

We combine the expertise of licensed therapists with cutting-edge AI technology to deliver comprehensive, personalized care that adapts to each individual’s unique needs and schedule.

Since our founding in 2025, we’ve supported over 50,000 individuals on their journey toward better mental health — and we’re just getting started. This platform was created by Abhilekh Borah, with a vision to make mental health care more compassionate, modern, and accessible for everyone.
